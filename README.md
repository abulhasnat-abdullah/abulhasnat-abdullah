## Research Interests

**Kinodynamic motion planning** — RRT\* and sampling-based planners extended with velocity bounds, torque limits, and inertial dynamics. Real-time replanning in dynamic environments where geometric planners fail.

**Autonomous mobile robotics** — Robust SLAM and localization for GPS-denied environments. Multi-sensor fusion: LiDAR + IMU + Camera into coherent world models. Long-horizon navigation with semantic map representations.

**Industrial automation & HRC** — AGV systems operating safely alongside human workers. Task planning aware of human intent, collision prediction, and proactive replanning.

**Mars analog terrain navigation** — Traversability analysis and perception pipelines for planetary surfaces — loose regolith, sharp rocks, steep slopes. 3D terrain-aware localization for rover locomotion.
